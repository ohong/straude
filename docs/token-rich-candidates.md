## Quality Flags

- **A-tier source**: Major publication (WSJ, NYT, Bloomberg, Fast Company, TechCrunch), official careers page, or founder post on LinkedIn/X
- **B-tier source**: Job board listing (Ashby, Lever, Greenhouse, Wellfound), employee post, or niche tech publication
- **C-tier source**: Glassdoor review, secondhand report, or rumor
- **[VERIFY]**: Needs manual verification (attribution unclear, rumor, or quote context uncertain)
